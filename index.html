Our Story

Welcome to Shree Siddh Kala Alankar, where we breathe life into the timeless traditions of Maharashtrian heritage. Based in the historic city of Nashik, we specialize in the meticulous creation of 100% handcrafted jewelry that captures the royal essence of our culture.

Founded and curated by Sheetal Gadekar and managed by Siddhesh Gadekar, our collection is more than just ornament—it is a tribute to the craftsmanship passed down through generations.
